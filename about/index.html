About Mark Smith

Background

The majority of my career has been spent in the data networking industry. After obtaining a degree in Mathematics from the University of Durham, I worked in the UK for a systems integrator and network hardware manufacturer. In the mid-nineties I was one of the first engineers to obtain the Cisco Certified Internetworking Expert (CCIE) qualification which enabled me to move to the US where I began working for a network integrator based in the San Francisco East Bay. Between then and now the telecommunications and IT industries have transformed several times over, most recently with a transition from hardware to software-based intelligence in the network and data center domains. This has magnified the need for data networking specialists to augment their CLI-based skills with those of a software coder. I have developed several tools to assist with this augmentation. Most recently, the availability of very high performance CPU and GPU systems at a personally affordable level along with the explosion of data available from existing and new IoT-based devices has created a further demand for data science skills to be added to the network engineer’s armoury. In response to this, I am currently studying for a Masters in Data Science from the University of Notre Dame
